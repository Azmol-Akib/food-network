Chicken Flat Ball

Split Pea Dhal

Prepare all the Dhal ingredients as above. Take a medium sized saucepan and heat the vegetable oil, add the onions and garlic and cook until golden brown. Add the garam masala, chilli powder and turmeric, cooking for a further minute. Add the chilli and gently cook for another 30 seconds. Add the chopped tomatoes, vegetable stock and split peas. Bring up to the boil, turn down to a simmer and cook until the split peas are tender (usually around 30 minutes). You should have a thick consistency.

Melanzane Parmiga

Pre heat oven to near max temp. Slice aubergines and lay on oiled baking sheet. Brush each slice with olive oil and sprinkle with salt. Do not overlap so may need 2 or 3 trays or cook in batches. When oven is really hot, cook slices for approx. 15/20 mins until they have changed colour and slightly brown. (There is no need to fry slices or salt them). When all slices have been cooked set to one side and gather the rest of the ingredients and a pyrex/baking dish.
